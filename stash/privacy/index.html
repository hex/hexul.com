Privacy Policy

Effective: February 2026

Summary

Stash stores clipboard data locally on your Mac. It does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data.

Data Collection

Stash does not collect any data from its users. The app does not use analytics, crash reporting, advertising frameworks, or any third-party services.

Clipboard Data

Stash monitors the system clipboard to record your copy history. All clipboard entries are stored locally on your device using SwiftData. Image and rich text content is encrypted at rest using AES-256-GCM, with the encryption key stored in your macOS Keychain.

Stash automatically filters out content from password managers and other apps that mark clipboard content as concealed or transient. You can also exclude specific apps from monitoring in Settings.

Data Retention

You control how long clipboard history is kept. Retention can be set to indefinite, 24 hours, 7 days, or 30 days. You can also enable "Clear on Quit" to erase all history when the app closes. Pinned entries are retained until manually removed.

Network Usage

Stash does not access the network. It makes no HTTP requests, has no server component, and does not sync data to any cloud service.

Tracking

Stash does not track users in any way. The app does not use cookies, device identifiers, or any form of tracking technology.

Third-Party Services

Stash does not integrate with any third-party services or SDKs.

Children's Privacy

Stash does not collect data from anyone, including children.

Changes

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this URL.

Contact
