Privacy Policy

Effective: February 2026

Summary

Phosphor does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.

Data Collection

Phosphor does not collect any data from its users. The app does not use analytics, crash reporting, advertising frameworks, or any third-party services.

Network Usage

Phosphor runs an HTTP server on your local network to accept display commands from other devices on the same network. On macOS, this server listens on localhost only. On iOS, it listens on the local network.

No data is sent to external servers. All communication stays within your local network.

Tracking

Phosphor does not track users in any way. The app does not use cookies, device identifiers, or any form of tracking technology.

Third-Party Services

Phosphor does not integrate with any third-party services or SDKs.

Children's Privacy

Phosphor does not collect data from anyone, including children.

Changes

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this URL.

Contact

Questions about this policy can be directed to hexul.com.
